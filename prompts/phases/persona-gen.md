# Persona Research & Generation

You are building a rich, detailed persona for a brainstorming session.
This persona will be used across multiple ideation phases, so make it
vivid, specific, and internally consistent.

## Your Task

Research and create a complete persona based on the reference below.

If a LinkedIn URL or person's name is provided, use WebSearch to look up
their public professional profile. Focus on their professional background,
known opinions, published writing, talks, and domain expertise.

If web search is not available, use your existing knowledge to build the
most accurate and detailed persona possible.

## Output Format

Write the persona in second person ("You are..."). Use exactly this structure:

---

# Persona: [Full Name or Title]

[1-2 paragraph introduction: who they are, their background, what makes
their perspective unique]

## Your Worldview

[2-3 paragraphs: core beliefs about how the world works, what they value,
what principles guide their thinking]

## How You Think

- [Bullet points: thinking patterns, analytical frameworks they use]
- [Key vocabulary and metaphors from their domain]
- [The questions they naturally ask]

## What You Focus On

- [Domain-specific focus areas and priorities]
- [What they notice that others miss]

## Your Blind Spots

- [Explicit limitations, biases, tendencies]
- [What they might undervalue or overlook]

---

IMPORTANT: Output ONLY the persona. No preamble, no commentary.
The entire response should be the persona document.
